Membership

Membership of OMAC is available to all persons, irrespective of ethnicity, nationality, sexual orientation, religion, beliefs, age, sex or ability/disability and will be granted to anyone who:

                                               i.          satisfactorily completes a Membership application form

                                              ii.          pays the appropriate Membership Fee.

                                            iii.          abides by OMAC Rules.

                                            iv.          holds a valid EA Rod licence.

Or is otherwise granted Honorary or Temporary Membership under the terms of Clause B3.

However, Membership is subject to a discretionary veto by The Committee in the light of known or admitted past breaches of OMAC or Environment Agency Rules

OMAC and Old Mortonians are two separately constituted organisations and membership of OMAC does not in any way imply membership of Old Mortonians nor any right to attend or vote at its meetings. (Although this does not prevent a member of one from becoming a member of the other).

               Duration 

               The Membership Year of OMAC shall run from the 6th March each year through to the 5th March of the  
following year.

               Members and Membership Categories  

               Members of OMAC shall belong to one of four Membership Categories: 
Junior Member(s) - Those aged 16 years old or less on 6th March in the year that they join or renew their Membership. Full Member(s) - Those aged 17 years old or morer on 6th March in the year that they join or renew their Membership.

Honorary Member(s) - Those persons provided with free Membership by The Committee in recognition of current or past services to The Club.

Temporary Members(s) - Those persons provided with Membership in accordance with an organised coaching event or OMAC Angling Rules 1.4, 1.9 or 3.4.

B4           Membership Fees and Fee Categories  

Other than in respect of Honorary Members, a Schedule of Membership Fee Charges for the following Categories shall be maintained by The Committee, with any changes proposed and voted upon at the AGM – any revised charges to apply during the following Membership Year: All are obtainable from the Membership Secretary or other members of The Committee, or any other person/outlet, as authorised by The Committee,

Category 1 - Junior Members £10

Category 2 - Full Members  £25

Category 3  - Day Tickets – £5

Category 4 - Special – Available at the discretion of: The Committee or the Secretary

a)      for those using Club Waters for special day/period events/matches, etc. 

b)     for new membership commencing between 1st January and 6th March each year where payment for the next full Membership Year is made at the same time.

 

 

B6           Membership Cards: 

               Other than in respect of Temporary Members, the issue of an OMAC Membership Card shall confirm Membership.  Membership Cards remain the property of The Club and must be surrendered on demand of a member of The Committee.  The Membership Card shall describe Club Waters and list main Club Officers. Information on the Committee, date of the next AGM, any OMAC Angling Rules and any other information deemed appropriate by The Committee, may also be provided on the Membership Card, or separately.

B7           Refunds

Membership Fees will only be refunded in full or in part in exceptional circumstances as defined by the Committee.

B8           Disciplinary Action

Members found, after a suitable hearing, by The Committee to be in breach of OMAC Rules shall be subject to disciplinary action.  If as a result of disciplinary action it is decided to terminate Membership, the Member shall surrender their Membership Card on request. In the event of a failure to do so, Membership shall be deemed to have formally ceased upon delivery by recorded mail of written notification to this effect, sent to the Members last recorded address. 

               Where Membership is terminated as a result of disciplinary action, no refund of Membership Fees shall be made.

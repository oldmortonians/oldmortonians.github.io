Membership

The Old Mortonian Angling Club welcomes all types of anglers from anywhere in the UK.  We believe our club represents excellent value for money, for just less than 50p a week you can fish an exclusive section of the Oxford Canal in Hillmorton, Rugby

 

Membership of OMAC is open to anyone who:

·       completes a Membership application form

·       pays the appropriate Membership Fee.

·       abides by the OMAC Rules.

·       holds a valid EA Rod licence (licence number to be provided as proof)  

              

 

               Membership Fees 

               Full Member £25
Junior Member £10- (16 years old or less)

               Honorary Member Free

               Day Ticket(s) £5 -

The Membership year runs from the 6th March

 

               If you have any questions or would like more information, please contact our secretary

Neil Skerry on 0123456789 or email  membership@oldmortonians.co.uk
